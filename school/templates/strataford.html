Extra Curriculum Activities

Each week, students have the opportunity to visit the library to explore new books and develop their love for literature. They get a chance to borrow reading books. Learners have an opportunity to participate in private swimming lessons which take place twice weekly at Constantia Park Swimming School. The lessons are designed to help your child be water safe, develop their swimming skills and promote self-reliance in a fun and safe environment. Our foundation phase learners have an opportunity to participate in weekly YSports private lessons which are designed to provide your child with valuable gross-motor skills and enjoyment in a structured environment.

Yearly Activities
  • Sports day;
  • Day outings;
  • School camp;
  • Adventure walk;
  • Concert.
