WE EMPOWER, ENRICH and ENABLE


The school was Established in 2013. Our morals and values are based on Christianity. We use the inclusive approach and offer small classes from babies (6 months) to Grade 7. For the past 11 years the school has been running and achieving its goals.

ABOUT US


We have a unique approach to learning, offering small classes and intervention programs to support the CAPS curriculum and ensure learning readiness. We create a supportive and engaging learning environment using smartboard technology and our approach ensures that every student enjoys learning while benefiting from one-on-one support and innovative tools. We offer a nurturing, home-like environment where children have the opportunity to be the best that they can be.


  • We are a family, we LOVE, LEARN, LIVE and LAUGH..
  • We provide learners with sense of belonging.
  • Offer an open, happy, welcoming environment.
  • Learners are physically and psychologically safe.
  • Learners are always motivated.
  • The staff gives it all and are devoted to their duties..

Our Vission

Empowering learners to fully develop their talents, understand themselves and the world and respect the beliefs and abilities of others..

Our Mision

We strive to offer an inclusive learning environment where every student, regardless of their background or abilities, is valued and empowered to reach their full potential. We are dedicated to providing personalised, high-quality education that supports diverse learning needs and promotes a culture of respect, collaboration and growth for all..

Our Interventions

Our intervention programs are designed to provide targeted support for each child’s unique needs and establish a firm foundation by supporting the neuro-developmental systems on which we build life skills. These programs support body-brain integration by strengthening neural pathways through consistency, rhythm and sequences and our Rainbow Dux Private School TLC Kid Care (From 6 Months to RR) Stratford Preparatory School (From Grade R-7) personalised instruction and resources help to address specific academic or developmental challenges. Our tailored guidance promotes their strengths, addresses areas for improvement and fosters a positive learning experience.
